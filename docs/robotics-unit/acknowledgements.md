# Agents

I wrote this as an experiment in how far I could go with generative AI, while maintaining my own personal narrative and style. The general flow was a mix of github copilot for structuring and site deployment and filling in skeleton text (slop), chatgpt for mathematics and images. In general, I had to do the least amount of editing to the maths and reasoning and the most for style, tone and philosophical opinions - the model defaulted to giving good technical snippets, but losing the fundamental connections between these. By far controllable image generation was the most frustrating for me, and I found this a useful exercise to reflect personally on how far we have come in our pursuit of 'intelligence' and how far we have to go.

I include extracts from a chat around the frustrations of generating a geometrically correct, but aestheticly pleasing rendering of the geometric meaning of a 2D rotation matrix. I think this is instructive from a robotics perspective, when we think about what we are trying to achieve in terms of building robots that can execute complex tasks from multimodal sensor streams.
